Refund & Cancellation Policy

Subscription cancellations, shipment refunds, and payout holds.

Effective: 8 April 2026 Platform: AfroRoute · afroroute.com Contact: support@afroroute.com

Purpose

This policy explains how AfroRoute handles subscription cancellations, subscription refunds, shipment-related cancellations, shipment-related refunds, and payout-related holds.

Subscriptions

AfroRoute offers paid subscription plans including Pro and Business.

Paid subscriptions are billed immediately at checkout. The Free Plan is the trial experience. Paid plans do not include a free trial unless clearly stated at checkout.

Subscription cancellation

You may cancel your paid subscription through your account settings or the Stripe Customer Portal.

Cancellation prevents future billing but does not automatically refund the current billing period unless required by law or approved by AfroRoute. After cancellation, your account may be moved to the Free Plan and Free Plan limits may apply.

Subscription refunds

Subscription fees are generally non-refundable once the billing period begins, except where:

  • Required by applicable law
  • Duplicate or incorrect billing occurred
  • A technical billing error occurred
  • AfroRoute approves a goodwill refund
  • The service was unavailable in a way that justifies refund review

Refund requests must be sent to billing@afroroute.com.

Consumer withdrawal rights

Where applicable consumer law grants a cancellation or withdrawal right, AfroRoute will honour rights required by law. If you request immediate access to paid digital services and begin using paid features, your refund rights may be affected to the extent permitted by applicable law.

Shipment-related payments

Shipment-related payments, where supported, are subject to manual review and payout release rules. A payment or delivery confirmation does not automatically guarantee payout release if there is a dispute, fraud concern, prohibited item concern, chargeback risk, or policy violation.

Cancellation before agreement is signed

If no shipment agreement has been signed, users may cancel a shipment request or trip interaction subject to Platform rules. No payout should be released before required agreement and delivery conditions are met.

Cancellation after agreement is signed

After both parties sign a shipment agreement, cancellation may require review. AfroRoute may consider whether pickup occurred, whether the Traveler incurred costs, whether the Sender changed or misrepresented items, whether an item violated policy, and whether there is an open dispute.

Refunds for shipment disputes

Refunds may be considered where:

  • The Traveler did not pick up the package
  • The package was not delivered
  • The package was materially damaged due to Traveler misconduct
  • The agreement was cancelled for valid reasons
  • Payment was made in error
  • Fraud is confirmed
  • AfroRoute determines a refund is appropriate

Refunds may be denied where:

  • The Sender provided prohibited or misrepresented items
  • The Sender failed to cooperate
  • The package was delivered and confirmed
  • Dispute evidence is insufficient
  • The claim is late or abusive
  • The user violated Platform rules
  • Payment has already been released and cannot reasonably be recovered

Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback, AfroRoute may suspend your account, pause related shipments, withhold payout, and provide evidence to payment processors.

Please contact AfroRoute support before filing a chargeback so we can review the issue.

Refund method

Approved refunds are usually returned to the original payment method where possible. Processing times depend on the payment provider and bank.

Contact

Refund and cancellation requests: billing@afroroute.com
