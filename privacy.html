Privacy Policy

How AfroRoute collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

Effective: 8 April 2026 Platform: AfroRoute · afroroute.com Contact: support@afroroute.com

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how AfroRoute collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal data when you use the Platform. By using AfroRoute, you acknowledge that your data will be processed as described here.

Data Controller: AfroRoute · 1200 Brickell Ave Suite 1950, PMB 70279, Miami, FL 33131, United States · privacy@afroroute.com

Personal data we collect

Account data — name, email address, phone number, country, profile photo, bio, language preference, account settings, subscription plan, and verification status.

Identity verification data — AfroRoute uses Stripe Identity to process verification. Stripe may collect identity document images, liveness data, verification results, and related metadata. AfroRoute stores limited verification status data such as session ID, status, verified timestamp, and basic metadata. AfroRoute does not intentionally store raw identity document images in public storage.

Listing and shipment data — trip details, shipment request details, item descriptions, photos, declared value, pickup and delivery cities, recipient details, agreement status, delivery confirmation status, and dispute records.

Messaging data — conversations, message content, timestamps, sender and recipient IDs, and listing references.

Payment and subscription data — Stripe customer ID, subscription ID, subscription status, billing plan, payment event metadata, and limited payment records. We do not store full card numbers.

Payout preference data — preferred payout method and instructions you provide. Payout details are private and used only for approved manual payout administration.

Technical data — IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, login events, security events, and error logs.

How we use personal data

We use personal data to create and manage accounts, provide marketplace functionality, verify identity, enforce subscription limits, process payments, support agreements and delivery confirmation, support payout review, process disputes, prevent fraud, comply with legal obligations, communicate service updates, provide support, and improve the Platform.

Legal bases for processing

Where EU/EEA or similar data protection laws apply, we process personal data based on:

  • Contract performance
  • Legitimate interests — fraud prevention, safety, platform operation, and dispute resolution
  • Consent where required
  • Legal obligations
  • Establishment, exercise, or defence of legal claims

Sharing personal data

We may share personal data with other users where necessary for marketplace interaction, Stripe for payments and identity verification, Supabase or hosting/database providers, Resend or email service providers, analytics and infrastructure providers, legal and professional advisors, and authorities where legally required.

We do not sell personal data.

Public and member-only visibility

Logged-out visitors may view public marketplace listing information, but AfroRoute hides profile photos and personal user images from logged-out visitors.

Sensitive information such as email, phone number, WhatsApp, payout details, identity documents, and verification records must not be publicly displayed.

Cookies and local storage

We may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies for login sessions, language preferences, security, remembering user state, analytics, and performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not work correctly without them.

Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as reasonably necessary for account operation, marketplace functionality, agreements and delivery records, payment and tax records, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, legal compliance, and security.

Shipment agreements, delivery confirmations, dispute records, payment records, and verification status records may be retained after account closure where necessary for legal, safety, or dispute purposes.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational safeguards to protect personal data. No system is completely secure. You are responsible for protecting your login credentials and notifying us of unauthorised access.

International transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your country of residence by service providers such as Stripe, Supabase, Vercel, and Resend. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data, receive data portability, withdraw consent, and lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@afroroute.com. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

Children

AfroRoute is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new effective date.
